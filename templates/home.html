Cartiv

The first collaborative shopping cart.

Cartiv makes shopping for your family or roommates simple. Each member of your household can add whatever items they want to a shared grocery list so that you don’t have to juggle all their requests. When you’re ready to go grocery shopping, you can notify your family or roommates through the app so they have time to include last-minute additions to your shared cart.

Stop fielding half-composed lists in your phone’s Notes app when Cartiv can handle your family’s shopping needs!
